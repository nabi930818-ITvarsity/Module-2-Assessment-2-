MARKHAM


Welcome to Markham at Pine City Mall!


Markham has become synonymous with the best in men’s fashion for generations.


Whether you are shopping for work clothes, casual wear or sportswear, Markham has a wide selection on offer.


The denim range is anchored by Markham-owned Relay Jeans and complemented by a denim selection from G-Star, Guess and Levi’s.


To complete any outfit, there is also a large variety of shoes and other accessories available, such as belts, glasses and jewellery. Markham also stocks a range of designer fragrances.
