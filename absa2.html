ABSA BANK


Welcome to Absa Bank at Pine City Mall!


Absa Bank, a wholly owned subsidiary of the Absa Group, offers a range of retail, business, wealth management, corporate and investment solutions to customers and clients across South Africa.


Our ambition is to sustainably create shared value and play an integral role in our customers’ and clients’ life journey, empowering them to achieve more and to recommend us to family, friends and business associates.


Absa is a truly African brand, inspired by the people we serve and determined to be always brave, passionate and ready so that we can make Africa proud.


Absa Group Limited is listed on the JSE and is one of Africa’s largest diversified financial services groups with a presence in 12 countries across the African continent, as well as offices in the United Kingdom and the United States.
