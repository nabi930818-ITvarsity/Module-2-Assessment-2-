Welcome to Sheet-Street at Pine City Mall!


Bringing you affordable, quality homeware must-haves is what we do. We want everyone to have the home of their dreams.


31 years down the line and our vision has remained constant. Although we have evolved our range and the spread of items, we still aim to offer tasteful products at exceptional value, allowing our customers to create the home they love, on a budget they can afford.


Creating dream homes also means offering you the right homeware for you. Understanding what you need and what you’d like, and giving you loads of options to choose from. Your home should feel like a piece of you, and we’re here to help make that happen wherever we can.


This is where heart and home come together.
