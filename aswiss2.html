AMERICAN SWISS


Welcome to American Swiss at Pine City Mall!


American Swiss has been one of South Africa’s leading Jewellers since 1896 when they opened their first store in Cape Town. For more than over 100 years American Swiss has been growing their business and now have stores throughout Southern Africa.


With over 240 stores across Southern Africa, American Swiss brings effortless style to those seeking fine jewellery that’s out of the ordinary.
