22 JUMP STREET


Welcome to 22 Jump Street Indoor Entertainment Centre.


Situated at the Pine City Mall.


It offers kids and adults alike entertainment for the whole family.


Let your imagination go, Trampolines, Foam Pits, Climbing Walls, Basket Ball Courts, and Olympic Wall Jumping Sections.


While you are waiting have some fun on state of the art bumpers cars and our games zone section.


22 Jump Street can be enjoyed by just about anyone, of any age, shape and physical ability.
